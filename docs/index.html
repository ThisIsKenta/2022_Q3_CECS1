Unlearning

  1. To undo the effect of; put aside the practice of. [1]
  2. To discard, put away, or get rid of (what one has learned); forget the knowledge of. [2]
  3. To forget, as what has been learned; to lose from memory; also, to learn the contrary of. [3]
